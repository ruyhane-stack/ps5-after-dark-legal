Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 21 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PS5 After Dark Studio handles information when used as a private creator workflow for gameplay clips, gaming reviews, Shorts, analytics, and social publishing integrations.

1. Information collected

Depending on which integrations are enabled by the account owner, the workflow may process:

  • Basic connected-account information, such as display name, profile links, account identifiers, and profile metadata.
  • Creator account statistics, such as follower counts, likes, following counts, video counts, or similar analytics made available by the connected platform.
  • Public video information, such as titles, captions, video IDs, URLs, thumbnails, publishing status, and performance information.
  • Creator-provided gameplay recordings, edited clips, captions, scripts, thumbnails, avatar overlays, and related media assets.
  • OAuth tokens or API credentials required to operate authorized integrations.

2. TikTok data use

For TikTok integrations, PS5 After Dark Studio may use approved TikTok scopes to confirm the connected creator identity, read profile details, read account statistics, review public videos, and, if approved by TikTok and authorized by the account owner, upload or publish video content. TikTok data is used only for the creator’s own content workflow.

3. How information is used

Information is used to:

  • Connect and verify creator accounts.
  • Prepare, organize, edit, and publish gameplay videos and Shorts.
  • Review prior posts and performance metrics for content planning.
  • Generate scripts, captions, hooks, thumbnails, avatar overlays, and other creator assets.
  • Maintain secure authorized access to third-party creator platforms.

4. Data sharing

Information is not sold. Data may be sent to third-party platforms only when necessary to operate integrations authorized by the account owner, such as TikTok, YouTube, Meta/Facebook, HeyGen, hosting providers, or other creator tools used in the workflow.

5. Storage and security

OAuth tokens, API credentials, and integration files are stored in restricted local secrets folders where possible. Reasonable safeguards are used to limit access to authorized operators. No security system is perfect, and the account owner should revoke credentials if access is no longer needed or if compromise is suspected.

6. Data retention

Creator workflow data is retained only as long as needed for editing, publishing, analytics, and operational purposes, unless the account owner chooses to retain project files for future use. Tokens may be retained until revoked, expired, or replaced.

7. User control and revocation

The account owner can revoke connected-platform access through each platform’s account or developer settings. Revoking access may disable related features such as reading account data, uploading videos, or publishing content.

8. Children’s privacy

PS5 After Dark Studio is not directed to children and is intended for use by the account owner or authorized adult operators.

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the workflow, integrations, or platform requirements change. The effective date above indicates the latest version.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or platform data use, contact the PS5 After Dark Studio account owner or administrator.
