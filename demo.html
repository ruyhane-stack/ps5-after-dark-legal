Upload gameplay Shorts to TikTok

Owner-operated web/desktop workflow for connecting the creator’s TikTok account and uploading edited gameplay clips for final review by the account owner.

Login KitOAuth connects only the owner’s TikTok account and confirms identity.
scope: user.info.basic
Content Posting APIUploads edited gameplay Shorts for owner review.
scope: video.upload
1
Connect TikTok

Owner starts OAuth from the PS5 After Dark workflow.

GET /oauth/start
scope=user.info.basic
redirect_uri=http://localhost:8765/callback/
2
Confirm account

The app reads basic profile only to confirm the connected creator account.

GET /v2/user/info/
identity confirmed
owner account only
3
Upload clip

Edited gameplay video is uploaded to TikTok using Content Posting API.

POST /v2/post/publish/inbox/video/init/
scope=video.upload
clip=gameplay-short.mp4
